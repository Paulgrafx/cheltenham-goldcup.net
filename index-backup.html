Cheltenham Festival

The Gold Cup takes place on the final day of the 2013 Cheltenham Festival and is the feature race on Friday.

Even with a superb racecard for each of the four days, each has its own feature race. The first of these is the Champion Hurdle odds which is usually the fourth race on the Tuesday.

Grand National

Following quickly after Cheltenham, comes the most popular race of the season. Aintree plays host to the forty Grand National Runners in what is always an exciting race.
